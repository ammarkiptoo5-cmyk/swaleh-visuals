08 — FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

What is included in each package?
+
Each package includes logo design, delivery files, and the revision rounds listed. Higher tiers include source files (AI/PSD), social media kits, and a brand style guide. Full details are in the pricing section above.
How do I pay?
+
Payment is via M-Pesa or bank transfer. A 50% deposit is required to begin, with the balance due on final delivery. Full upfront payment earns a priority project slot.
What if I need changes after delivery?
+
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. Additional revisions beyond the limit are billed at KSh 200 per round. The Gold package includes unlimited revisions throughout the entire project.
Do you offer custom quotes?
+
Absolutely. For larger systems, multi-page documents, or ongoing retainers, contact me via WhatsApp or the form below. I will respond with a tailored proposal within 24 hours.
How fast can you deliver?
+
Delivery ranges from 1–2 days (Gold/Rush) to 7 days (Starter). Rush delivery is available on any package for an additional KSh 500. Timelines are always confirmed before work begins.
What file formats will I receive?
+
All deliveries include PNG and PDF. Silver and Gold tiers also include source files (AI and PSD), giving you full ownership and the ability to edit or print at any scale.
